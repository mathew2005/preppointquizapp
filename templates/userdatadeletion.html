User Data Deletion

Last Updated: 17/06/2024

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Preppoint, please follow these steps:


1. Log in to Your Account: Visit Preppoint and log in to your account.


2. Access Your Profile: Once logged in, navigate to your profile by clicking on your profile picture or username.


3. Delete Your Account:
- Go to the account deletion page by visiting: www.preppoint.co/user/delete.
- Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion of your account.


Once you have confirmed the deletion, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed from our system.
If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact us at: preppointer@gmail.com or message us on telegram @preppointsupport
